WHY WE SIGN
我們為何連署
Why We Sign

台灣的道路，正在奪走人命。不是偶爾，而是每一天。

過去十年，我們聽過太多「加強宣導」、「駕駛人不守規矩」的說法。 但數據告訴我們：個人行為不是核心問題，道路設計才是。 當系統允許高速行駛穿越住宅區，當自行車道只剩地面白漆， 當行人被迫與機車共用人行道——人命傷亡是可預測的結果，不是運氣。

2026 年的地方選舉，是我們手中最直接的政策工具。 我們不要求候選人做到完美，我們要求他們承諾方向—— 以「2035 年交通死傷減半、2050 年零死亡與零碳排」為願景， 透過五項具體政策訴求，讓每一條台灣的街道，值得我們稱之為「家」。

請你加入連署，告訴每一位候選人：選票來自安全的城市。

Taiwan's roads are taking lives — not occasionally, but every single day.

For too long, we have been told that the problem is careless drivers. But the data is clear: individual behaviour is not the root cause — road design is. When the system permits high-speed traffic through residential zones, when bike lanes are nothing more than painted lines, when pedestrians share footpaths with motorcycles — casualties are a predictable outcome, not bad luck.

The 2026 local elections are our most direct policy lever. We don't ask candidates to be perfect — we ask them to commit to a direction: halve road deaths by 2035, reach zero deaths and zero carbon by 2050, through five concrete policy demands.

Sign with us. Tell every candidate: votes come from safe cities.

FIVE DEMANDS · 五大訴求
我們要求候選人承諾什麼
What We Demand Candidates Commit To
DEMAND 01 · 訴求一
LAW · 立法
立法保障
制定《雙零交通願景自治條例》
Legislative Protection
Enact the Dual-Zero Transport Vision Ordinance
推動地方立法，設立副市長層級以上統籌的跨局處轉型機制； 設置永續交通轉型基金，納入至少 50% 交通罰鍰收入及停車收益； 法制化專家與公民參與，確保決策透明。
Enact local legislation to establish cross-department coordination at deputy mayor level or above. Create a Sustainable Transport Transition Fund drawing at least 50% of traffic fine revenue and parking fees. Mandate expert and civic participation in transparent decision-making.
DEMAND 02 · 訴求二
SPACE · 空間
道路空間重分配
打造實體防護的主動運輸路網
Road Space Redistribution
Build a physically protected active transport network
於慢車道建置具實體庇護及遮蔭之自行車路網，推動連續無障礙人行道； 三年內優先完成大眾運輸場站及各級學校周邊之行人友善改造； 每年以增加 1–2% 公共運輸市佔率為執行目標。
Build a physically protected, shaded cycling network in slow-traffic lanes, alongside continuous barrier-free footpaths. Prioritise pedestrian-friendly improvements near transit stations and schools within three years. Target a 1–2% annual increase in public transport modal share.
DEMAND 03 · 訴求三
STREET · 街道
友善宜居生活街道
落實住宅區生活街道建設
Liveable Living Streets
Create residential streets built for people
界定轄區內穿越型道路與服務街道，於住宅區推動交通寧靜化改造； 導入路幅縮減、減速平台等實體降速設施； 推動大眾運輸導向發展（TOD），於場站周邊保障社會住宅比例。
Classify through-roads and local service streets across the district. Introduce traffic calming in residential zones: road narrowing, speed tables, and physical speed reduction measures. Advance Transit-Oriented Development (TOD) with guaranteed social housing near transit hubs.
DEMAND 04 · 訴求四
VOLUME · 總量
總量管理
以價制量與低碳管控
Volume Management
Price-based demand control and low-carbon regulation
訂定路外及路邊停車空間總量上限，將道路空間轉換為綠樹與自行車道； 任期內推動並指定「低碳交通區」； 協調中央推動燃油車輛數量管理，結合精進之駕駛訓練制度。
Cap on-street and off-street parking supply; repurpose freed road space for trees and cycling infrastructure. Designate Low-Carbon Transport Zones within the term of office. Coordinate with central government on fuel vehicle fleet management and improved driver training standards.
DEMAND 05 · 訴求五
WELFARE · 幸福
幸福交通
友善勞動通勤與企業獎助 · 不遺落任何人
Happy Commute & Welfare
Worker-friendly commuting, enterprise incentives, leaving no one behind
輔導並獎勵企業推動「綠色通勤獎勵制度」，補助員工大眾運輸及單車通勤費用，鼓勵彈性工時； 全面落實包容性設計，保障孩童、青少年、身障者、高齡者及各類多元使用者之通行權利， 確保交通轉型不遺落任何人。
Guide and incentivise businesses to adopt Green Commute Reward schemes, subsidising employees' public transport and cycling costs, and encouraging flexible hours. Embed inclusive design principles across all transport interventions — protecting the mobility rights of children, youth, persons with disabilities, older adults, and all users. No one gets left behind in the transport transition.
